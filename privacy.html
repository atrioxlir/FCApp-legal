Faith Connection Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2, 2026

Faith Connection is an event discovery and community connection app. This Privacy Policy explains what information may be collected, how it may be used, and how users can contact us with privacy questions.

Information We May Collect

Faith Connection may collect information that users or authorized event managers provide, such as names, email addresses, organization information, event details, event images, event locations, links, and other information needed to display or manage events in the app.

How We Use Information

We use information to operate Faith Connection, display events, manage event content, support user and admin features, improve app functionality, troubleshoot issues, and communicate with users when needed.

Event Content

Event information submitted through Faith Connection may be shown inside the app so users can view event details. Authorized users are responsible for submitting accurate event information and only uploading content they have permission to share.

Device and Usage Information

The app or related services may collect basic technical information, such as device type, app version, diagnostics, and usage information to help maintain, secure, and improve the app.

Third-Party Services

Faith Connection may use third-party services for app hosting, database storage, authentication, media, analytics, crash reporting, or app distribution. These services may process information as needed to provide their services.

Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information. We may share information only as needed to operate the app, comply with legal requirements, protect the app and users, or work with trusted service providers.

Data Retention

We keep information only as long as needed to provide the app, maintain records, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, or improve app operations.

Children

Faith Connection is not intended to knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child provided personal information, please contact us so we can review and take appropriate action.

User Choices

Users may contact us to ask questions about their information or to request updates or deletion where applicable.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a new “Last updated” date.
